HTML5 Music Player

There are only 3 main audio formats that are supported by HTML5. Those formats are FLAC, WAVE, MP3 and OGG. Matroska or WEBM also has some limited support it really depends on what the browsers can render. The safe route for most browsers with Audio is MP3.
