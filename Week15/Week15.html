Notes: CSS3 Animations Without Libraries

Animations are very useful tools because of one huge factor. You can make animations infinite and they can be easily called with javaScript making your code mush easier to use and understand. Such was the case with my animated mario sprite. There are multiple keyframe sets all for different situations.

Animations are also powerful in the fact that you can use them either to make tweens or play them in steps. Tweens are when you smoothly transition an objact from on state to another. Steps are when wou move or change your object in a jerky manor. Refer to my Mario Charecter if you would like to see stepping animations in action.
